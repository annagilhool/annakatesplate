Summer Salmon Salad

Ingredients


  • fresh 1/2lb salmon
  • smoked paprika
  • mixed greens
  • lemon
  • avocado oil (i use the whole foods spray)
  • balsamic vinegar
  • avocado
  • tomato
  • lime
  • cumin
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions


Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Place your salmon on a sheet of heavy duty tin foil. Spray with avocado oil and season with salt, pepper, and smoked paprika. Bring the two long ends of the tin foil together and enclose your salmon in the tin foil. Seal off the ends of the tin foil as well so the salmon is completely wrapped in the foil. Bake for ~17 minutes.


For the guac, mash an avocado in a bowl until smooth. Chop your tomatoes into small cubes and add your desired amount of tomatoes. Add the juice of half a lime. Season with salt, pepper, and cumin. Mix until everything is fully encorporated.


Dress your mixed greens with balsamic and lemon juice. Top off your greens with the salmon and a dollop of guac. Enjoy!
